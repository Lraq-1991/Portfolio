Cafe Sales - Exploratory Data Analysis¶

In the current notebook we will use the cleaned dataset produced in the previous one.

The objective is to apply different EDA techniques to extract valuable descriptive information from the data, explore trends and get some insight from visualizations.

This notebook is divided into three main themes: Sales Insights, Time-Based Patterns, and Customer Behavior.
